\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

This paper examines the effect of paid sick leave on analyst coverage. As shown in Table \ref{tab:main_results}, the coefficient on PAID\_SICK\_LEAVE is 0.034 and significant at the 1\% level.

We also find robust results in Table \ref{tab:robustness}. The results are consistent across different specifications.
